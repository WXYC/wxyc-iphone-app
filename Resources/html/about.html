WXYC 89.3 FM is the non-commercial student-run radio station of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. We broadcast at 1100 watts from the Student Union on the UNC campus, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Our coverage area encompasses approximately 370 square miles in central North Carolina.

Visit wxyc.org for more information. Please help keep freeform radio alive by donating to us here.
